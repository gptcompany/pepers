# Terminology Map: Samuel A. Lopes Research (CMUP / UPorto)

| Term / Notation | Definition / Context | Relevance in Lopes Research |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Ore Extension** | $A[x; \sigma, \delta]$ where $\sigma$ is an endomorphism and $\delta$ a $\sigma$-derivation. | Central structure in the 2025/2026 cohomology papers. |
| **Gerstenhaber Bracket** | $[ \cdot, \cdot ]$ defined on the Hochschild cohomology $HH^*(A)$. | Used for calculating Lie structures on cohomology rings. |
| **Poisson-Hopf Algebra** | A Hopf algebra with a compatible Poisson structure. | Topic of the 2025 research on Weyl algebra structures. |
| **Hochschild Cohomology** | $HH^n(A, M) = \text{Ext}^n_{A^e}(A, M)$. | The primary tool for studying deformations of algebras. |
| **Weyl Algebra** | $A_n(k)$, the algebra of differential operators with polynomial coefficients. | The playground for many of Lopes’s structural discoveries. |
| **Cauchon-Effros Property** | A property related to the distribution of prime ideals in quantum algebras. | Foundational work in Lopes's early career (e.g., 2023-2024). |

**Note on UPorto / CMUP Standards:**
The Centre for Mathematics (CMUP) emphasizes a rigorous categorical approach to non-commutative structures. Pay special attention to the transition from classical Poisson geometry to the non-commutative (Quantum) setting.
