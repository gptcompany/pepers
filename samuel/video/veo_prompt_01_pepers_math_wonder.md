# Veo Prompt 01 — PePeRS Brand + Math Wonder

Create an 8-second vertical premium animated short, 9:16.

Absolutely no text.
No subtitles.
No voiceover.
No letters.
No numbers.
No readable symbols.
No title cards.
No infographic overlays.

If image reference is supported, use the provided PePeRS logo as the primary visual identity reference.
Preserve the logo spirit:
- playful frog-like mascot energy
- arcade-meets-research tone
- deep indigo / purple background
- warm gold / amber accents
- cyan / electric blue highlights
- premium glowing edges
Do not turn it into a generic cartoon frog.
It must feel like the PePeRS mascot translated into a cinematic animated character.

Goal:
Make mathematics feel alive, beautiful, and for everyone.
Not childish.
Not academic.
Not generic.
The short should feel welcoming to all ages, especially curious young people, but still smart enough for adults.

Core visual idea:
A logo-inspired PePeRS frog-like geometric mascot enters a strange mathematical world.
The world looks complex at first, but hidden structure reveals that mathematics is not chaos:
it is pattern, symmetry, invariance, and elegant transformation.

This must feel mathematically rich without using readable formulas.
Use visual mathematics instead:
- symmetry axes
- rotational invariance
- mirror reflections
- geometric tilings
- orbit-like trajectories
- graph-like nodes and connections
- clean coordinate-like grids
- topological deformation of shapes
- controlled morphing that preserves deep structure

Timeline:
0.0s - 1.5s
The PePeRS logo-inspired frog mascot appears in a dark indigo space, glowing softly.
It lands lightly as if entering a game level or research lab.
Around it float elegant mathematical fragments:
grids, mirrored lines, rotating polygons, arcs, node-link structures.
No readable equations, only visual math.

1.5s - 3.5s
The mascot nudges one glowing shape.
That shape starts transforming:
circle to square to triangle to curved noncommutative abstract object.
The transformations are fluid and beautiful.
The mascot reacts with delight and curiosity, not childish exaggeration.

3.5s - 5.8s
A deeper hidden structure lights up behind everything:
symmetry lattice, tile geometry, invariant axes, rotational balance.
The mascot realizes that underneath the changing shapes, a stable mathematical order survives.
This is the emotional reveal.

5.8s - 8.0s
The mascot jumps or floats upward into the center of the mathematical structure.
Everything aligns into one radiant final composition:
the PePeRS mascot silhouette, surrounded by glowing symmetry, tiling, mirror lines, and balanced geometric motion.
The ending must feel like:
math is beautiful, alive, and open to everyone.

Style:
- premium motion design
- elegant and playful
- mathematically rich
- emotionally intelligent
- no childish slapstick
- no school blackboard cliches
- no generic AI sci-fi noise
- no random surrealism
- no clutter

Camera:
- centered composition
- one continuous fluid shot preferred
- slight push-in during the final reveal
- no shaky camera
- no chaotic cuts

Color palette:
- deep indigo / dark violet / near-black
- warm amber / gold highlights
- cyan / electric blue accents
- subtle cream-white geometry glow
- preserve the PePeRS logo palette feel

Animation:
- very clean silhouette readability
- smooth morphing
- subtle expressive mascot motion
- strong contrast for phone screens
- mathematical elements should feel precise, not decorative

Audio:
- refined playful synth pulses
- delicate glowing chimes
- one satisfying harmonic bloom when the hidden math structure reveals itself
- no comedy sound effects
- no cheap arcade noise

Negative prompt:
text, subtitles, letters, numbers, readable formulas, title cards, infographic style, generic green cartoon frog, childish mascot faces, meme humor, blackboard, classroom, stock education visuals, tourist Portugal imagery, flags, landmarks, chaotic edits, low-quality morphing, visual clutter, loud slapstick, generic sci-fi nonsense
