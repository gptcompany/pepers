## Milestone Summary

**Key Decisions:**
- Batch iteration loop with safety cap (100 iterations) over single-call approach
- is_nontrivial() heuristic over strict whitelist for formula filtering
- clean_latex() with 9 macro categories over selective stripping
- Integration tests with mocked services over pure E2E (deterministic, fast)

**Issues Resolved:**
- Paper stage stuck at "extracted" after validator/codegen
- >50 formulas never processed (batch limit 50, no iteration)
- Trivial LaTeX fragments (\alpha, ^{1}) stored as formulas
- \tag{N} treated as variable by codegen
- ~35% parse_latex failure rate

**Issues Deferred:**
- Pre-existing ruff/type errors in older test files (9 ruff + 10 type, not introduced by v9.0)

**Technical Debt Incurred:**
- `_update_paper_stage()` duplicated in validator and codegen (6 LOC each, acceptable per KISS)
